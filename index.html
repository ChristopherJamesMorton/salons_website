Hairdreams® MicroLines

Fuller hair, right where it's thinning

MicroLines is Hairdreams' gentle answer to thinning hair — an ultra-fine, breathable hair system with real human hair individually knotted in, attached to your own hair to add natural density. No surgery, no glue, no scalp adhesives, no daily removal. As an elite Hairdreams salon, we custom-fit, cut, and color it to disappear into your hair.

Undetectable

The ultra-fine base vanishes into your own hair — part it, style it, wear it up. No one can tell.

Gentle & non-surgical

Bonded to your existing hair, never your scalp. No heat, no tape, no chemicals — comfortable day and night.

Real human hair

Color-matched and cut to blend seamlessly. Sleep, shower, and style in it 24/7, and re-fit as your hair grows.
